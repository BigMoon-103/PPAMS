About the Project

Introduction

In today's world, people spend much of their day seated working, studying, or gaming. Over time, this leads to slouching, muscle strain, and longterm spinal problems. The Posture Pillow Attachment is designed to combat this by combining comfort, technology, and smart feedback. It transforms any ordinary chair into a posture aware system that helps users develop healthier sitting habits. In essence, it's a simple solution for one of modern life's most common health issues, poor posture caused by prolonged sitting.

Project Objective

The primary goal of this project is to create a sensor based ergonomic attachment that monitors a user's posture and sitting duration in real time. By tracking pressure points along the spine and shoulders, it helps identify unhealthy positions and encourages regular movement through alerts and feedback. Through both hardware and software integration, the system acts as a personal posture assistant, reminding users when to sit upright or take short breaks. Ultimately, it aims to reduce long term strain on the spine while promoting more mindful sitting behavior.
